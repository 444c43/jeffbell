About JBE

The Jeff Bell Experience is a professional DJ entertainment company that is known for having the most highly interactive entertainers in all of North East Florida. The owner has been in the industry for over 20 years and has continuously been one of the top Wedding DJs for over 15 years.


The Jeff Bell Experience has five DJs with a combined 50+ years of experience in DJ and entertainment. Our entertainers go through high levels of training consisting of Music Theory, Sound Engineering, Program Profiling, Strategic Musical Programming, and Psychology of Dance (understanding how to get people to dance). We are NOT the type of DJs who just play music. We are professional entertainer who have been trained to understand the psychology of how to breakdown a person’s insecurities and help them reach a level of celebration that very few ever have.


We specialize in performing for:


Weddings Bar/Bat Mitzvahs
Corporate Events Team Building Events
Proms Homecomings
Grand Openings Holiday Parties

The Jeff Bell Experience is one of the only professional DJ entertainment companies in all of North East Florida to hold perfect 5.0 out of 5.0 ratings on Facebook, Google, WeddingWire, and The Knot. The company is known for its high level of professionalism, organization, and entertainment value.


Our prices are based off the type of event, entertainer you choose and the number of hours you need entertainment. The best thing to do is contact us with the date and type of event and we will gladly send you a list of available entertainers and prices for each one.


Services

We will provide music and entertainment up to the total amount of the contracted time. We will handle all the music and announcements that are needed during the contracted time as well as work with and assist all other vendors in the coordinating, planning, and execution of the event before, during, and after.


Equipment

One complete mobile DJ sound system which includes: 2 high quality professional DJ Speakers, 2 wireless microphones, professional music mixing equipment that was made for the purpose of mobile DJing, and two intelligent "disco" lights.


Time

We typically arrive at least two to three hours before the start of the event to set up. This is at no cost to you. We prefer to arrive early therefore if there are unforeseen problems they can be corrected before the event begins. Overtime can be purchased the night of and the fee shall be agreed upon when the contract is signed.


Sound system Rental

We will handle all the music and announcements that are needed during the contracted time that can be provided from the single sound system mentioned above. If music and/or announcement are needed somewhere out of range of the aforementioned sound system a 2nd sound system may be rented. Fee depends on the amount of sound equipment needs


Music Downloads

For the protection of our equipment and our business we will only download music that can be purchased from reputable and established websites such as iTunes or Amazon. If any songs you request are not available on either site it will be your responsibility to provide those songs to your DJ. We will NOT illegally download music. This include YouTube.


Moneys

We require a non-refundable retainer equal to the cost of the first hour to secure the date. The retainer will be subtracted from the balance and the remaining balance must be paid no later than two weeks prior to the event. Payment may be in the form of cash, check, cashier’s check, credit card, or payment apps such as Venmo or Cash App.


Travel

We require additional travel fees for any event further than 60 miles from our location. A fee of $50 (subject to change) shall be paid for every hour of travel needed to reach the event.


Accommodations

If the event is over 60 miles away from entertainer(s) residence The Jeff Bell Experience (JBE) requires any and all clients who hire an entertainer(s) from JBE to provide accommodations for the entertainer(s) if entertainer(s) is not able to return to his/her residence by midnight of the event date by the same means of travel they arrived. JBE requires accommodations to be provided in a safe location in an establishment with a 3 star rating or higher. Accommodations must be approved by entertainer(s) and client both unless prior agreement is made and stated in contract.


We are licensed and insured in the state of Florida.
